Pickle Dip

An easy 3-ingredient that can be used with tacos or in a wrap.

Ingredients

  • 1 softened cream cheese
  • 6 dill pickle spears, diced
  • 1 package thinly sliced dried beef

Steps

  1. Mix together the softened cheese, pickles and chopped beef until well combined.
  2. Chill till ready to serve.
